Architecture — the control layer.

TVEVT is not a posting tool. It is infrastructure for signal governance and distribution authority: Draft → Governance → Execution Immutable Log. This page defines what the system is, what it enforces, and what “proof” means in v1.

Canonical doctrine

The Signal Journey is the product: drafting discipline, governance gates, and immutable execution proof. Anything that bypasses governance is “noise”, not infrastructure.

Infrastructure (not UX)

Rules, approvals, retention, auditability. UX is the interface; control is the product.

Embedded AI (operational)

AI strengthens preparation and policy checks. Not decoration. Not “auto-posting”.

Immutable execution log

A verifiable record: who executed what, where, and when — as a permanent standard.

1) The Signal Journey

Every output travels through three surfaces. This is the system boundary.

  • DraftingPreparation

    Message creation, tone discipline, channel-ready variants, and deterministic formatting rules.

  • GovernanceEnforcement

    Approvals, policies, role gates, and plan-based controls before anything can be executed.

  • Execution LogProof

    Append-only proof surface: channel + time + payload + status. No silent edits.

2) What “Governance Console” means

Governance is a control plane for authority: who can publish, what goes out, when and where.

Control surface What it enforces
Roles & permissions Owner / Editor / Approver gates. Prevents accidental or unauthorized output.
Approvals Single-step or multi-step approvals (by tier). Nothing goes out without passing gates.
Execution rules Channel constraints, allowed formats, scheduling windows, and policy checks.
Retention & proof Immutable log retention by plan. Auditability becomes an infrastructure standard.

3) Package limits (conceptual)

Operational tiers scale execution volume; strategic tiers scale enforcement, retention, and institutional control.

Plan class Primary scaling axis
Operational (monthly) Executions / AI credits / connectors / log retention depth.
Strategic (annual) Enforcement, cross-channel consistency, longer retention, governance depth, enterprise controls.
Enterprise+ SSO/SCIM, SLA, dedicated support, extended proof surface, custom policy layer.
Overage doctrine

Extra executions are billed ($1–$2 by tier). AI credits in packs ($20 / 100). Smart upgrade triggers when overage exceeds plan delta.

4) Execution Log — what makes it “proof”

The log becomes a verifiable record only if entries are append-only and tied to governance state.

  • Append-onlyImmutability

    Entries can be added, not removed. No silent edits. This creates institutional trust.

  • Proof surfaceVerification

    Execution becomes verifiable: output channel + time + payload + status.

  • Governance linkAccountability

    Approvals determine what reaches “sent”. Plans can remain “planned”. Failures remain recorded.

  • Audit mindsetRetention

    In v1.1 this maps to KV/DB (append-only) with retention by plan.
