What TVEVT is

A governance-first console that makes publishing authority measurable and enforceable. It is built for organizations where reputation is structural — and output must be controlled.

  • Control surface: roles, approvals, policies, execution rules.
  • Distribution authority: connectors are permissioned capabilities.
  • Proof: append-only execution log (no silent edits).

Who it is for

Teams that cannot afford “accidental publishing”, inconsistency, or ungoverned signal.

  • Investor relations & founders
  • Regulated comms & compliance-sensitive orgs
  • Multi-team brands and agencies
  • Security / IT-controlled publishing environments
